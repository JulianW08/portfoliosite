Hey — I'm Julian, an 18 y/o Italian and Nigerian incoming Computer Engineering student at UIUC, and I've been pointing cameras at interesting things since sophomore year of high school.

My mind tends to run in multiple directions at once. Turns out that's useful when you're composing a shot — you notice everything. The texture of a wall. The way light bends around a corner. The stranger crossing the street.

This site is where photography meets engineering. Right now it's a portfolio. Eventually it'll be a playground.
